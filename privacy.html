Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Travel Adventure Finder ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you create and use an account, we collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Unique user ID
  • Profile information (languages, travel preferences, travel style)
  • Visited countries and bucket list countries
  • Friends connections and mutual travel data
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information strictly to provide and improve app functionality, including:

  • Authenticating and managing user accounts
  • Displaying your travel profile
  • Enabling social features such as friends and mutual destination comparisons
  • Storing and displaying user-generated travel data
  • Maintaining platform security and reliability

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider to securely store and manage user data.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information while your account remains active. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account at any time within the app by navigating to Settings and selecting "Delete Account".

Account deletion permanently removes:

  • Your email address
  • Profile data
  • Visited and bucket list countries
  • Friends connections
  • Uploaded profile photos
  • Associated identifiers

Deleted data cannot be recovered.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections, to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Travel Adventure Finder is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
