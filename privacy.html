Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Travel Adventure Finder ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create and use an account:

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Profile information (languages, travel style, preferences)
  • Visited countries
  • Bucket list countries
  • Friends connections
  • Profile photos (if uploaded)

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Provide profile and social features
  • Enable visited and bucket list tracking
  • Support mutual travel comparisons between friends
  • Maintain app functionality

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider to securely store and manage user data.

Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account remains active.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time within the app by navigating to Settings and selecting "Delete Account".

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your email address
  • Your profile data
  • Visited countries
  • Bucket list countries
  • Friends connections
  • Uploaded profile photos
  • Associated identifiers

Deleted data cannot be recovered.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure infrastructure to protect your data.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: review@travelaf.app
